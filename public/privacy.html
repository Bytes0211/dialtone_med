Privacy notice

This is a privacy notice for DialTone.Med. If anything here is unclear, email hello@bytestreams.ai.

This policy explains what information DialTone.Med collects when you call or text a healthcare practice that uses our service, submit information through this website, or otherwise interact with our patient-communication tools. DialTone.Med is an AI-powered phone assistant built for healthcare providers.

01 What we collect

  • Phone number. When you call a participating practice, we receive your caller ID from the phone network. We use it to route follow-up messages and help practices recognize returning callers.
  • Voice call content. The AI assistant may transcribe and temporarily retain call content so it can handle scheduling, billing questions, refill requests, insurance checks, or message-taking workflows. Recordings or transcripts may be retained short-term for quality review and dispute resolution.
  • Patient-submitted details. Information you provide during a call or form submission, such as your name, callback information, appointment preferences, insurance details, and the reason for your request.
  • Practice workflow data. Status information associated with a request, such as whether an appointment was booked, a message was routed, or a follow-up task was created for staff review.
  • Website usage. Standard web server logs, including IP address, user agent, and requested page. We do not use tracking cookies on this marketing site.

02 How we use it

  • To answer, route, and summarize inbound patient calls for participating healthcare practices.
  • To support scheduling, billing questions, prescription refill requests, insurance verification, and message-taking workflows.
  • To send transactional SMS messages such as appointment confirmations, callback updates, or secure follow-up instructions.
  • To let the participating practice review and act on the patient request.
  • To debug, monitor, and improve the service when something goes wrong on a call or message flow.

We do not sell your information. We do not use caller or patient-submitted information for third-party advertising. We use data only to operate and improve the service for the participating practice.

03 Service providers

DialTone relies on a small number of third parties to operate:

  • Supabase — database and secure application data storage.
  • Twilio — phone number routing and SMS delivery.
  • Vapi — the voice agent platform (speech-to-text, large language model, text-to-speech orchestration).
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech voice generation used by Vapi.
  • OpenAI — large language model powering the voice agent.
  • Resend — delivery of website contact-form email notifications.
  • Cloudflare — web hosting and application delivery.

04 SMS messaging

If you interact with a practice through DialTone.Med, we may send transactional SMS messages on the practice's behalf, such as appointment confirmations, callback updates, intake reminders, or secure follow-up links. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on the workflow involved.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out may limit the practice's ability to send confirmations, reminders, or follow-up instructions by text.

We do not use the phone numbers we collect for marketing SMS, and we do not share them with other companies for their marketing.

05 Data retention

Request records and operational logs are retained for the participating practice's business, compliance, and support needs. Voice call transcripts and recordings may be retained short-term for quality review, troubleshooting, and audit support, then deleted or archived according to applicable workflow and retention requirements.

06 Your rights

You can ask us to confirm what information we hold that is associated with your phone number or submitted contact information, to correct it if it is wrong, or to delete it, subject to the participating practice's own legal and record-keeping obligations. Email hello@bytestreams.ai and we will respond within a reasonable time.

07 Children

DialTone.Med is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them through this marketing site.

08 Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where feasible, notify affected customers or participating practices. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09 Contact

Questions or privacy concerns: hello@bytestreams.ai.
